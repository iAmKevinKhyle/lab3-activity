About CvSU - Silang Campus

Cavite State University - Silang Campus is committed to providing quality education and shaping future professionals equipped with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for global competitiveness.

Our History

In early 2006, the local government of Silang received numerous requests from the community to establish a branch of Cavite State University. Responding to this need, a Memorandum of Agreement was signed on April 18, 2006 between Mayor Clarito A. Poblete and University President Dr. Ruperto S. Sangalang.

This agreement marked the official establishment of the CvSU Silang Campus, aimed at bringing accessible and quality higher education closer to the people of Silang and nearby communities.

Academic Programs

CvSU Silang Campus offers a variety of undergraduate programs designed to meet industry demands and equip students with practical and theoretical knowledge.

  • Bachelor of Science in Business Management
  • Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management

Mission and Vision

Mission

Cavite State University shall provide excellent, equitable and relevant educational opportunities in the arts, sciences and technology through quality instruction and responsive research. It shall produce skilled and morally upright individuals for global competitiveness.

Vision

The premier university in historic Cavite globally recognized for excellence in academics, research, innovation, and community engagement.

Core Values

  • Truth
  • Excellence
  • Service
  • Integrity

Campus Facilities

The Silang Campus is equipped with modern classrooms, computer laboratories, a library, and spaces for student activities. These facilities support both academic learning and personal development.
