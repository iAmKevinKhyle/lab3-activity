Event Details

Event: Cavite State University - IT/CS Workshop 2026 Venue: CvSU Silang Campus Gymnasium Date: October 1, 2026 Time: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM The IT/CS Workshop 2026 is a one-day academic and hands-on event designed for students who are passionate about technology and innovation. This workshop will feature interactive sessions on modern topics such as web development, cybersecurity, networking, and software engineering. Participants will have the opportunity to learn from experienced instructors, engage in practical coding activities, and collaborate with fellow students. The event also aims to enhance problem-solving skills and introduce current industry trends in Information Technology and Computer Science. Activities include:
- Live coding demonstrations
- Hands-on mini projects
- Tech talks from invited speakers
- Networking with IT/CS students and faculty Who can join? Open to all CvSU students, especially those enrolled in BS Information Technology and BS Computer Science programs. Registration Fee: Free (Limited slots only) Secure your slot now and be part of this exciting learning experience!
